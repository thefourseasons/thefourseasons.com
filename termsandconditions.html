Section-A (Sponsor operations section)

This section includes terms and conditions for sponsors and their rights according to their privileges. The below explain every term with reference to other.

Terms & Conditions



  • It is mandatory for the sponsor to pay at least 80% of the total amount of the sponsorship packet taken by the sponsor.

  • The sponsor has privileges & is not allowed to exceed those.

  • Collaterals if any are to be brought in by the sponsor representative a week before the event.

  • Extra banners if any or stands are not allowed unless the management grants permissions for them & should contain ''The Four Seasons'' logo.

  • If extra branding material is brought in by the sponsor it is at his own cost & shall contain ''The Four Seasons'' logo on it.

  • Refund of the sponsorship amount is only possible if any sponsor privileges violated.

  • The amount spent on branding, ad's & promotions is to be deducted from the refundable amount.

  • If the sponsor wants a refund duplicate copies of the bills shall be provided to the sponsor so as to gain a brief idea of the expenses spent on their advertising/branding that includes banners & all other printed materials that have a logo of the sponsor on them.

  • If the sponsor needs a separate promotional live Ad at the event (such as a dance performance or a song of the company) charges for that are to be paid to the dancer's/singer's directly & a deal has to be signed for the same.

  • If any amount of sponsorship packet is pending (max 20% can be pending) it has to be paid immediately by the sponsor after the event within 2-3 business days if not possible a specific time shall be given to the party and if the amount is not paid lawful actions shall be executed.

  • Any damage to the venue or ''The Four Seasons'' property by the sponsor or any executive of the sponsor shall result into them paying for the fixes on spot.

  • If due to some external reasons event does not take place the cost spent on advertising can't be refunded as the branding has been fulfilled rest of the amount shall be refunded to the sponsor account within 5-7 working days.

  • Payments are to be made through cheque only.

  • To avail extra privileges extra charges have to be paid. Negotiations are possible.

  • As verification the conversations between the sponsor & organisers or ''The Four Seasons'' representative shall be recorded.

  • The sponsorship agreement shall be signed before paying ''The Four Seasons'' for the event.

  • The agreement should contain a stamp of the sponsor with a sign. Organizers hold every right at the event.





Section-B (Volunteer Operations section)

This section includes certain rules and regulations for the volunteer so as to gain an idea of the maximum possible powers they hold at the event.

Rules & Regulations

  • The foremost thing a volunteer should keep into account is that organisers hold every legal right at the event.

  • Any decisions cannot be taken own one's own will prior permissions from the organisers shall be granted.

  • The section (e.g. security) for which the volunteer is held responsible should not be handed over to another volunteer.

  • Volunteers should hold their respective places as assigned and shall not violate the given tasks.

  • Volunteers are allowed to question any suspicious activity going on at the event before or after.

  • ID cards shall be carried with as it will be used as a security clearance key.

  • Volunteers should attend the crowd calmly.

  • If in case a volunteer is not able to come work load will be shifted equally.

  • Volunteers have to attend every meeting before the event to plan everything properly to avoid mistakes at the time of the event.

  • Volunteers shall take part in the pre-event promotions as well.

  • Volunteers database shall be revised before the event to gain maximum possible time to understand the event flow.

  • Apart from event duties volunteers are responsible for analyzing the crowd and different classes in it.

  • A report shall be given to the organizers for the same.

  • A pre planned list shall be made and ensured one day before the event and should be readily available at the time of the event.

  • Volunteers hold the right to gain maximum possible information about the event except for some internal databases of the company. Organizers hold every right at the event.





Section-C (Artist operations section)



This section primarily includes the terms and conditions for the artists so as to maintain a friendly and a peaceful environment and to avoid issues before or after the event. By these we can ensure a proper flow of the event preparations.

Terms & Conditions



  • Artist shall be open to suggestions & shall behave calmly with the organizers & the crew including volunteers. Aggression without any specified reason shall not be tolerated.

  • If due to any external reasons artist or a group of artists cancel a performance at their own will the amount of that performance shall be deducted from the initial advance payment (if any) or the final amount to be handed over to the artist at the end of the event.

  • If required, advance payment shall be given to the artist by signing a confirmation form for advance payment & handing it over to the authorities.

  • The maximum possible advance payment an artist can get before the event is 40% of the total payment to be made after the event.

  • The artist signing a deal shall not perform at any event 9 hours prior to our event or else strict actions can be taken against them.

  • If the above rule is violated & there is a loss at the event the artist is to be held responsible.

  • To process the artist payments the management shall take 2-4 working days to process the amount of payment & upload data to the servers & verify the bills.

  • Any promotions other than the listed sponsors & the management are prohibited.

  • If there is any change in the performance prior permissions of the CEO shall be taken before executing any action on stage or off stage.

  • In case the CEO is not present due to some reasons permissions shall be granted by the CFO to proceed further.

  • Before the event all artists shall submit their content so the authorities can review it before proceeding further.

  • Rehearsals are compulsory for all the artists.

  • Artists are responsible for their own belongings. In case of any loss of or to their property the management is not to be held responsible.

  • Artists shall strictly follow the database given to them

  • Any damage to venue or ''The Four Seasons'' property by the artist or a group of artists shall result into them paying for the fixes on spot. Organizers hold every right at the event.
